Life in Athens

My life here in Athens is SO much more fun than I ever could have imagined! I have made so many great friends through my sorority and have had countless exciting adventures. Some of my favorite memories include going to football games on Saturday (Go Dawgs!) and aimlessly wandering through downtown Athens with friends. There are so many things to do in Athens, that it is virtually impossible to be bored. Whether it be shopping, eating, or listening to live music, Athens, GA has something for everyone!

Before you get too overwhelmed by all the options on the website linked below, I will tell you some of my favorite places around town. First things first, food! If you're in the mood for some great pizza, definitely check out Transmetroplitan or Mellow Mushroom. Feeling sushi? Try Shokitini. Mexican? Cali N Titos or Agua Linda are your best bets! My favorite shops are Impeccable Pig, Fringe, Urban Outfitters, and Entourage. Finally, my favorite places to listen to music are the Georgia Theater and the Foundry.
